Key Features

Language Resources

01

Dictionary & Vocabulary

A comprehensive dictionary for various languages/dialects of the Cordillera (e.g., Kankanaey, Ibaloi, Ifugao, Bontoc, etc.). Include audio pronunciations, common phrases, and translations.

02

Grammar Guides

Simple grammar explanations with examples and interactive exercises.

03

Cultural Context

Explanations of how language ties to cultural practices, rituals, and beliefs specific to each language group.

Audio & Video Lessons

04

Learn by Listening

Audio recordings by native speakers for language immersion (pronunciation, intonation, and conversational practice).

05

Video Lessons

Native speakers teaching common phrases, greetings, and storytelling.

06

Interactive Quizzes

After lessons, users can take quizzes to reinforce their learning.

Storytelling Hub

07

Traditional Stories & Folklore

08

Language Community

11

About Us

We are a team dedicated to preserving and promoting the native languages of the Cordillera region. Our mission is to make these languages accessible to everyone, from learners to native speakers, and to help enhance language learning in schools through interactive resources and cultural engagement.
